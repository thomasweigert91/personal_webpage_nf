Welcome to my personal website. I am an aspiring web developer and I am looking forward to exciting tasks and new challenges.


About me

Hello! My name is Thomas, I am 31 years old and live near Hamburg.

Already in my youth it was always my wish to work in the field of web development one day. However, my path led me in a different direction at that time and so I still reached my goal via some detours. In October 2022 I started a bootcamp at the company NeueFische to become a Web Developer. I wanted to use this chance to give myself the opportunity to dive into the field of programming and make my childhood dream my profession. What appeals to me about this job: I find it exciting how varied and creative this profession can be. There are always new challenges and problems to solve. And that's exactly what I enjoy!

Outside of work, I enjoy being out in nature with my two dogs. Half a year ago, I also found a passion for baking bread.

Where i have worked

Schooling and education


After my Abitur at the BBS 2 in Stade, I completed my training as a banker in 2010. At a local savings bank I learned everything about banking, insurance, credit and shares.


Professional career


My professional career began at the savings bank where I completed my training. My duties mainly included advising private customers in all areas of finance. In search of a new challenge and personal development, I switched to the credit area in 2015.

At two online platforms for personal loan comparisons I worked as a specialist for personal loans until 2020. My main tasks included advising private and business customers on personal loans. Together with my customers, I searched for a suitable bank according to their personal situation and individual wishes and applied for the desired loan together with my customers.

At the beginning of 2021, I took a slightly different direction and switched to the area of real estate financing. The tasks were identical to my previous ones, but the spectrum of real estate financing was much more complex. My tasks mainly included supporting and advising my customers, checking files and valuing properties, and corresponding with partner banks.
